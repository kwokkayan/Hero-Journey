Level 1: Tutorial
Welcome to the Demon King's Dungeon!

There are 5 levels in the dungeon and each level has its unique challenges.
You will also encounter some monsters.
Your goal is to defeat the Demon King at the end.

Level 1 (this level) will be a tutorial which helps you understand different objects and monsters in the dungeon.
Let's start by walking to the first room!

By the way, You can receive instructions by touching rectangles with a '!' (just like this one).
